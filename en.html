Surroundings

The Tisa Walls

The Tisa Walls is a popular destination for tourists and climbers, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape and interesting climbing routes of varying difficulty.

Decinsky Sneznik

This is the highest sandstone table mountain in the Czech Republic, stands at 723 m above sea level, providing beautiful views of the surrounding landscape.

Ore Mountains

The mountains, located northwest of the Tisa, offer summer activities as cyclist and winter activities like skiing and cross-country skiing – for example Telnice is 20 minutes drive.
